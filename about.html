Prompt

“How will you use generative AI or blockchain technology to change banking for good? Reimagine the future of banking by leveraging generative AI or blockchain technology to solve real-world challenges like financial inequality, climate action, or customer trust. Propose solutions that make banking more accessible, sustainable, or human-centric. Think beyond today. Could generative AI or blockchain technology be used to invent new ways to bank like voice-driven interfaces for the elderly, gamified saving tools for Gen Z, or AI tutors for underserved communities? Prioritize ethics, inclusivity, and scalability to prove that innovation and social good can go hand in hand.”

Increase Financial Accessibility

Increase Financial Accessibility: Use AI to provide a low-cost banking service to underserved communities.

Enhance Customer Trust

Enhance Customer Trust: Leverage generative AI to reduce fraud and build trust through secure transactions.

Promote Financial Literacy

Promote Financial Literacy: Use generative AI to create personalized financial education tools, helping people make informed decisions.

Why We Chose This Prompt

We thought that we could make a practical solution to the prompt that would have a widespread effect on people of all backgrounds. We saw that this solution would benefit underserved communities by combatting their lack of financial literacy and knowledge of money. We believe that by utilizing generative AI, we can create a solution that could change lives and guide people to making better financial decisions.
